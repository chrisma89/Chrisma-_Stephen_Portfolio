About Me

Hello, I'm Chrisma. I'm a dentist turning into a software developer. While i have enjoyed being a dentist, in my heart i knew that i wanted to find a new passion to pursue. I took up some basic coding lessons and i fell in love with the magic that it was. The predictability, the creative thinking and planning that goes behind the scenes and then the delivery of a user friendly webpage was enough to draw me towards web development. So, here I am! From shaping pretty composites onto teeth to shaping a teeny tiny portion of the world wide web.
