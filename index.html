About Me

Hi, welcome! I have been working as a dentist for several years now and my heart wanted a new challenge to pursue. I have heard a lot about software development through my sisters and gave it a little try. I enjoyed it more than I thought I would. The predictability of coding, the creative flare that i could bring, and the constant need to learn new technology was more than enough to persuade me to delve deeper into software developing. So here I am, making the change - from shaping pretty composites onto teeth to shaping the world wide web.
